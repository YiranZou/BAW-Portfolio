Zou Yiran

Zou Yiran(b. 1995, China) is a multimedia artist. Currently living in San Francisco, she mixes social commentary, popular aesthetics, references to surrealism in her films and installations. Her works reflect on people and the city she lives in. In the current research direction, she still focus on the study of the rapid changes that are occurring in Chinese society today.

Education

MFA California College of the Art, San Francisco, CA, USA

BFA Central Academy of Fine Art in Digital Media Art, BeiJing, China
